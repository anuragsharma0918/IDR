About IDR

The Institute for Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute specifically designed for digital, cyber, and technology risk roles.

IDR is an industry-led training and deployment institute dedicated to preparing individuals for digital and cyber risk roles. Our mission is to bridge the skills gap and create a pipeline of talent for the rapidly evolving digital landscape.

We operate at the intersection of critical infrastructure, financial services, and highly regulated industries where the stakes are highest.

Through our exclusive UK university partnerships, we bridge the gap between deep academic insight and real-world project delivery. Our unique hybrid model ensures that practitioners do not just learn the theory—they immediately apply it to solve high-consequence enterprise challenges.
